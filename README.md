## About Me

Software developer with a constant curiosity for understanding how things work and how to make them better. Over the years I've worked across backend, frontend, cloud, and blockchain — and I've found that what I enjoy most is diving deep into whatever a project needs to move forward.

I take a technology-agnostic approach, always prioritizing the most effective solution for each case. What drives me is solving real problems, designing clear solutions for complex environments, and growing with every challenge. I care about building things that work well and working with people who push each other to be better.
